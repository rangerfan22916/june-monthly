Our Story

Little Bird Toys, established in 2018, is a family-run business dedicated to creating handcrafted wooden toys that spark imagination and encourage slow, meaningful playtime.

Our heirloom-quality collection is made from sustainable Baltic birch wood and other premium hardwoods, designed to last for generations.

We take pride in our eco-friendly manufacturing processes and our commitment to quality, safety, and timeless design.

Visit our store in Toms River, NJ to experience the magic firsthand and meet the team behind Little Bird Toys.
