Our Story

Founded in 2018, Little Bird Toys began as a heartfelt dream to bring families together through the timeless joy of handcrafted wooden toys. As a family-run business, our mission has always been to create toys that inspire creativity, imagination, and slow, meaningful playtime. In today’s fast-paced digital world, we believe in the power of simple, durable toys that encourage children to explore, learn, and connect with their surroundings in a mindful way.

Each piece in our heirloom-quality collection is carefully crafted using sustainable Baltic birch wood and other premium hardwoods, selected not only for their natural beauty but also for their durability. Our toys are designed to withstand the test of time, becoming cherished keepsakes that can be passed down through generations. We believe that every toy should tell a story and hold a special place in a child’s life, inspiring wonder and discovery for years to come.

At Little Bird Toys, we are deeply committed to eco-friendly manufacturing practices. From sourcing responsibly harvested wood to minimizing waste and using non-toxic, child-safe finishes, we prioritize sustainability at every step of our process. Our dedication to quality and safety means parents can trust that our toys are not only beautiful but also built to the highest standards, providing a safe and enriching experience for children of all ages.

Our passion for creating meaningful toys is matched only by our love for community. We invite you to visit our cozy store in Toms River, New Jersey, where you can see and feel the magic of our handcrafted toys in person. Meet the dedicated team behind Little Bird Toys and learn more about our craft, values, and the stories that inspire each creation. Whether you’re looking for the perfect gift or a timeless addition to your child’s playroom, we are here to help you celebrate the art of play.
